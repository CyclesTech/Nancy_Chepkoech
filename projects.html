Projects & Research

Project Report on Herbarium at Nairobi Museum

Description: This project involved extensive research and fieldwork at the Nairobi National Museum's herbarium, where I helped in identifying, preserving, and cataloging plant specimens.

Areas of Focus: Plant taxonomy, specimen preservation, data collection, and biodiversity conservation.

Project Report on Marine Organisms at Kanamai Beach, Mombasa

Description: Conducted a detailed study on marine biodiversity at Kanamai Beach, Mombasa, focusing on species identification and environmental interactions of marine organisms.

Areas of Focus: Marine biology, biodiversity conservation, species documentation, and environmental sustainability.

Factors Contributing to Poor Performance in Kericho Schools (Kapkormom School Sample)

Description: This research aimed to investigate the underlying factors contributing to academic underperformance in selected schools within the Kericho region, using Kapkormom School as a case study.

Areas of Focus: Educational challenges, socio-economic factors, school infrastructure, and student motivation.
